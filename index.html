FITC Token FAQs

Your guide to the most common questions about the FITC Token

Who is Natty?

Natty is a natural fitness meme character born in May 2024. He aims to achieve an exceptional physique without steroids, promoting natural fitness and a healthy lifestyle within the crypto community.

What is the purpose of $FITC?

$FITC aims to create the largest crypto fitness community. It supports launching free-access gyms for token holders, expanding the community on a large scale, and creating a circular economy with referral codes, merchandise, and more.
